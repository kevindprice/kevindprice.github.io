My experience with physics

I do not have a physics degree, but I have studied physics-mechanics successfully three times in school.

I originally studied physics as a high school student. The next year, I studied physics again as an advanced placement student.

In college, I ended up taking another similar course, “Principles of Physics I, for Scientists and Engineers,” due to some school requirements.

In none of these classes was the Coriolis effect ever mentioned. I was never given a physics problem similar to this one. I cannot remember where I originally read about the Coriolis effect, but this subject is something that has fascinated me for years.
